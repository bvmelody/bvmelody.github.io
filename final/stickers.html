Stickers!

This is the easiest collectible to display in my opinion, especially since they stick to nearly anything. I've been collecting stickers for arguably the shortest amount of time, but compared to the other two it is the absolute easiest to start collecting and getting a large collection quickly! Stickers are commonly given away for free at outreach events, networking, job fairs, and all sorts of other places/events! It's not uncommon for people in IT to have their entire machine decked out in all sorts of nerdy stickers!

Inexhaustive list of Sticker Types:

Category Summary Difficulties of Collecting
Normal The normal run of the mill sticker that you can find being given away for free or in Sticker books, easiest to find and most numerous. Very easy to personalize anything with a small pile of them. Not super difficult as they are readily available both online and all over the place in brick and mortar stores.
Holographic Sticker A more expensive sticker that has a holographic film over the main part of the sticker. Often used to add a touch of changing color to a display. Easy to find online, both stickers designed for safety and for cool factor.
Textured Stickers Stickers that have non-flat textures to them, can be both for fun like on a display, or practical, like on a Rubik's Cube. Easy to find online, including specialist stores for purpose built textured stickers for objects.
Vinyl Stickers Also known as PVC stickers, these stickers are known to be much more durable and long-lasting compared to their counterparts. It is often used for displaying on objects that regularly receive abuse such as a Laptop, tools, or safety equipment. Second most common type of sticker and readily available on Etsy and Amazon. Honestly I recommend this one the most.

Why should you collect stickers?

Stickers are arguably the easiest of the three examples to collect. Being cheap to produce and widely available whether as freebees or for purchase! They are also rather easy to slap onto nearly anything from clothing to notebooks. It's also much easier to purchase custom stickers than a custom enamel pin or a custom patch.
