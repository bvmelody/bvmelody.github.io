Why Collect?

Collecting anything can be both a obtuse mess, expensive, and often times can require a lot of space. For many this can be both a blessing and a curse, after all, collecting anything can provide a creative outlet and a way to spend one's time in short bursts over a long period. But one thing I've noticed during my time is that some forms of collecting are more practical for some people than others. For example, my hallmark, pin trading is incredibly practical to my needs. Mainly that it is inexpensive compared to other things, and is very easy to show it off. But in many ways I have expanded since then, now starting a sticker collection and even an embroidery patch collection.
