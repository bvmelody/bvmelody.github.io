Patches!

These are the collectible that are the best for fabrics, especially if you are really into music. Patches have been around for centuries, first primarily as a way to help mend clothing holes, especially large ones, by adding a new strip of fabric over top of said hole.

Over time this allowed for new processes with cloth patches being used for unit and rank identification in militaries around the world, most notable starting in the world wars. From there it made the jump into niche music communities where punks would add band patches and cloth album covers to jackets in order to express themselves.
